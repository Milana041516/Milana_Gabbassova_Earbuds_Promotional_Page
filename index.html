Section FAQ

FAQ

Are the LoveBeats earbuds available in different colors?

Yes! LoveBeats earbuds come in multiple colors, allowing you to choose the perfect shade to match your style. You can select your favorite color option during the customization process.

Do the LoveBeats earbuds have noise-canceling features?

LoveBeats earbuds are designed with passive noise isolation, which helps reduce background noise for a clearer listening experience. Although they don't have active noise cancellation, their snug fit minimizes external sound.

How long does the battery last on a single charge?

LoveBeats earbuds offer up to 6 hours of listening time on a single charge, and the portable charging case provides an additional 18 hours, giving you a total of 24 hours of playtime.

Are the LoveBeats earbuds compatible with both iOS and Android devices?

Absolutely! LoveBeats earbuds connect seamlessly with both iOS and Android devices via Bluetooth, so you can enjoy your favorite music on any platform.

Are the earbuds comfortable for long listening sessions?

Yes, the LoveBeats earbuds are designed with a lightweight, ergonomic shape and come with different sizes of ear tips to ensure a comfortable, secure fit for extended wear.
